Welcome to Ayebilla Avoka Data Science learning page.

This is a page for my weekly technical blogging, mostly on Data Science and Technology. I find that, writing is the best way to learn and communicate our ideas and skill. So, I have chosen to do it anyways, whether good or bad with one goal in mind: My ability to communicate technical information will not remain the same a year from now with constant practice! If you find errors or think there is something I can do to improve my skills and grow faster, you are most welcome. If you find a reason to laugh at my naivety, please do so honorably but remember, next year you will come back here to learn new skills from here. Enjoy the rest!

Who am I?

I am a fresh graduate from a renowned university in Africa with a degree in Computer Engineering. I have decided to do a 'brute force learning' this year so that I can get as much skills and experience as I need to succeed at work and grad school. I love technology and have never been satisfied of discovering new things and unlearning old things. I hope to get a job after a few months of practice and thereafter begin my freelance journey so that I can have some time to build my company.
